About Us

How the Art App came to be.

November 1, 2015 Jane Doe

The team behind The Art App came together when we realized an issue in our society: people were creative, but they didn't have a reliable place to learn how to use their talent. Thats were the app came in. We created a site where people can get a membership and find private craft tutorials and instructions for projects. It is appropriate for artists of all ages, of all experiences, though beginners may find it the most helpful. Members can also submit their own projects, tutorials, and instructions to show to other users.
